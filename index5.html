Frequently Asked Questions

Alternative medicine refers to any practice that aims to achieve the healing effects of medicine but which lacks biological plausibility and is untested, untestable or proven ineffective.

When practiced by a trained professional, most alternative therapies are very safe. We conduct a thorough intake to ensure our treatments are appropriate for you and your family's health conditions.
